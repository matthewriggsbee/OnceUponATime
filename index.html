We are a childcare center in Brownsville Tennessee. We were started in August of 1996 by a mother needing a place to take her children. We have remained in the same location, 405 Dupree St, Brownsville, TN 38012, ever since.

We have classes ranging from the infant rooms to an after school care for children up to seven years old. There is and infant room for 12-30 months, a toddler room for 30-42 months, and a preschool room for 42-60 months. There is also an afterschool program for children aging between 5 and 7 years old.
